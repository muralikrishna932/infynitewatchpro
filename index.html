Complete Security Solutions

01

CCTV Installation

Professional on-site assessment and full installation of IP, analog, PTZ, and HD cameras tailored to your property layout.

  • HD, 4K & night-vision systems
  • DVR / NVR setup & cabling
  • Customized coverage planning
02

Maintenance

Scheduled preventive maintenance to keep your cameras and security systems performing at peak efficiency year-round.

  • Regular system health checks
  • Lens cleaning & realignment
  • Firmware & software updates
  • Monthly & annual plans available
03

Configuration

Expert setup of recording schedules, motion detection zones, alerts, remote access and smart analytics for your system.

  • Remote viewing setup (mobile/PC)
  • Motion zones & alert triggers
  • Cloud & local storage config
  • Smart AI detection features

Repair & Fault-Fixing

Fast diagnosis and repair of faulty cameras, damaged cables, recorder failures, connectivity issues and power problems.

  • On-site fault diagnosis
  • Camera & DVR/NVR repairs

  • Same-day emergency service

Round-the-clock live monitoring with real-time alert notifications, incident response and detailed reporting dashboards.

  • Live remote monitoring center
  • Instant mobile notifications
  • Incident logging & reporting
  • Monthly analytics summaries

Access Control

Intelligent entry management using key cards, biometrics, PIN pads and intercoms — integrated with your CCTV system.

  • Biometric & card reader systems
